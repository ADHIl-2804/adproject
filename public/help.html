Steps for booking the token

Step:1 Navigate to Booking: Click on the "SLOT BOOKING" link in the navigation bar or the "Book Your Slot Now" button on the Home page.
step :-2 Enter Your Details: Fill in your Full Name, Email, and a valid Mobile Number in the registration form.
step :- 3 Select Date & Time: * Choose the date you plan to shop. Pick a specific 10-minute time slot (e.g., 10:10 AM) from the dropdown menu.
step :- 4 Generate Your Token: Click the "GENERATE TOKEN" button. This will redirect you to the QR Code page.
step :-5 Save Your Token: On the QR page, your unique code will appear. Click the "Download your token" button to save the digital pass to your device. otherwise it will lose

Rules and Guidelines

>>>Punctuality is Key: Your booking is valid only for the specific 10-minute window selected. Please arrive at the billing counter exactly at your booked time.
>>>One Token Per Person: Each token is valid for a single person and a single billing transaction.
>>>Show Your QR Code: You must present the downloaded QR code (either as a digital image or a printout) to the staff at the priority counter.
>>>Grace Period: If you are more than 5 minutes late for your slot, your token may expire, and you will be required to join the regular queue or book a new slot.
>>>Accuracy: Ensure your mobile number and email are correct, as these details are embedded in your QR token for verification.
>>>Fair Usage: Please do not book multiple slots for the same day unless necessary, to allow other shoppers to benefit from the system.
>>>You cannot book the slot with same details for same time
